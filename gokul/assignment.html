James Francis Cameron

James Francis Cameron is a Canadian filmmaker and environmentalist who is best known for making science fiction and epic films for the Hollywood mainstream.He was born on August 16, 1954 kapuskasing, Ontario, Canada. His feature directorial debut was the 1982 release Piranha II: The Spawning.The next film he directed was the science fiction action thriller The Terminator in 1984. It starred Arnold Schwarzenegger as the titular cyborg assassin, and was Cameron's breakthrough feature. In 2010, Time magazine named Cameron one of the 100 most influential people in the world.
